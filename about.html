ABOUT

The port of the smiling coast, whose smile brightens the african continents. welcoming incoming ships giving outgoing ones pleasant and unforgettable memories. yes, Gambia Ports Authority, twenty nine years or more you are now. May God bless you.

Some call you the Authority of fame, Others call you the Gambia Ports Authority, But i call you the Authority of progress, success and goodwill.

Throughout the trial and tribulation of growth you evolved from the marine department to the Gambia Pots Authority. The first banjul port project, The second banjul port project, The third banjul port project, All in pursuance of becoming africa's port of singapore.

To workers of the Gambia ports authority, The task is enormous, And the opposing wind could be varying, But being in the forefront of the Gambia's economic Development Progress. We must strive, For if we do, There is victory ahead for us all
